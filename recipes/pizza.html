Description

Homemade pizza should be a traditional practice.

Pizza blends in every occasion, be it a formal or informal event, a home snack or quick grab-and-go meal, it fits.
Let's explore a simple recipe on how to make pizza.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup all purpose flour
  • Tomato ketchup and 1 tomato
  • 2 onion
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoon olive oil
  • Cheese
  • 4 mushrooms
  • 1/2 green pepper
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella
  • 1 tablespoon dry yeast
  • Enough warm water

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough and add all-purpose flour to a kneading plate.
  2. Then add baking powder and salt and mix it a bit.
  3. Next add your olive oil, then the yeast and sugar.
  4. Mix all well with warm water and wait for about 3-5 hours for your pizza dough to be ready.
  5. Prepare the pizza base
  6. Switch your oven to reach 180 degrees Celsius first.
  8. Take your your rolling pin or a clean thick cylidrical rod and dust it with dry flour, and
  9. Include the space where you will put your pizza dough.
  10. Roll your dough into nice circular base or any shape of your like.
  12. Make little holes in your dough using a fork so that it can be flat and not puffy when done.
  13. Put it into oven for about 15 minutes.
  14. Chop all the vegetables for your pizza.
  15. Spread the sauce and vegies on the prepared pizza base.
  16. Bake the pizza at 250 degrees C for 10-15 minutes.
